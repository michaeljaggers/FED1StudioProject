Why Choose Us?

Today's technology

Our class offerings cover the top technologies, and methodolgies used today by big tech corporations like Google, Facebook, Twitter and more. You can be confident upon completion of our courses that you will have the keys to a successful career in graphic design, no matter where you work.

Top Talent

Kansas City Design Institute has acquired top-talent in the industry to serve as your mentors throughout the program. Each of our instructors has been in the field a minimum of five years and has had previous experience in the field of running workshops, public speaking, and furthering their craft. You can be confident that here you are learning from the best.
