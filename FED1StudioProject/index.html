About Us

Founded in 1992, The Kansas City Design Institute has produced thousands of the best graphic design professionals in the industry. Students from all geographic areas (and walks of life) have found themselves at our doors; passionate about taking their skills to the next level. With an education from KCDI, you get more than a strong foundation to start your career. You will receive training from the best in the business, build contacts, and get exposed to ideas you never thought possible.

Why Choose Us

Today's technology

Our class offerings cover the top technologies, and methodolgies used today by big tech corporations like Google, Facebook, Twitter and more. You can be confident upon completion of our courses that you will have the keys to a successful career in graphic design, no matter where you work.
